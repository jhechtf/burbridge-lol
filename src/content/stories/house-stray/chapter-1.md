Like all major cities Volhabende was a sprawling place, filled with all sorts. The city stretched from the ports of a deep bay in the west
 to hilly section off to the east. The wealthy of Volhabende chose to live in that hilly section, as it offered a view of the setting
 sun and let them look out over the hustle and bustle of a more quiet town.
